# Marklet

![Marklet Icon](images/icon128.png)

Save and revisit exact text from any webpage. Highlight, bookmark, and instantly jump back to what matters.

## Features

- **Jump Back to Context**</br>
Reopen the page and automatically scroll to and highlight your saved snippet.

- **Save Exact Text**</br>
Highlight any text and store it as a bookmark — no clutter.

- **Privacy First**</br>
All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is tracked or sent anywhere.


## Future Enhancements 

- **Sync across devices & Cloud Storage** - Access your highlighted snippets anywhere securely.
- **Tags & Folders** - Organize your saved text snippets into categories and custom folders.
- **Search & Filtering** - Quickly find specific highlights by keyword, date, or website domain.
- **Export Options** - Export your highlights seamlessly to Notion, Obsidian, Markdown, or CSV.
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** - Instantly save and highlight text using fast custom keybindings.
- **Annotations & Notes** - Add your own personal comments and thoughts context to your saved snippets.
- **Shareable Links** - Generate links that instantly open and scroll others to the exact highlighted text.
